# Code of conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and constructive project. Participation must remain respectful regardless of
experience, identity, background, nationality, language, disability, appearance, religion, or personal views.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and evidence rather than attacking people.
- Give actionable feedback and accept correction professionally.
- Respect privacy, attribution, licenses, and confidential reports.
- Avoid harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, doxxing, spam, and deliberate disruption.
- Help keep technical disagreements focused on the project's goals and documented constraints.

## Scope

This code applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, release channels, and public spaces where
someone represents the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content, close or lock conversations, reject contributions, issue a warning, or
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior threatens the community or project.

For a private conduct report, contact the maintainer through a private method listed on the
[@Favoritchannel](https://github.com/Favoritchannel) profile. If no private method is available, open a minimal public
issue requesting a private channel and do not include sensitive details. Reports will be handled as confidentially as
practical, with conflicts of interest disclosed and avoided.
